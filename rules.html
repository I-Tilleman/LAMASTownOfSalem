Introduction Model Knowledge Rules Progress Code

General Inference Rules

  1. If there are n other players and you know of n-1 of the players that they are not Mafia then the player of whom you do not know whether they are Mafia is Mafia.

Role-Specific Inference Rules

Lookout

  1. If you see that players visit x and x is dead, one of the players that visited x is mafia and none are a doctor.
  2. If you see that players visit x and no one is dead, one of the players that visited x was mafia and one was a doctor.
  3. If you see that players visit x and y dies then the players visiting x were not mafia.

Doctor

  1. If there is no veteran in the game and you got the message that you succesfully healed x, you know that x is not mafia.

Escort

  1. If the doctor is not in the game and there is no death, the player x who you distracted is the godfather.
  2. If there is no veteran and there is a death or if the veteran died, the player x who you distracted is not mafia.

Godfather

  1. If you receive the message that you got attacked and were healed during the night, you know that the player x who you attacked during the night is the veteran.
