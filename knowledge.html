Simplification

The game we are modeling is quite complex in terms of information and knowledge. In theory, we could model the agents' knowledge of the roles of agents and of the actions of agents during the night. This further complicated by the important roles that belief and lying play in this game. We have decided to only model the agents' knowledge of the roles of agents and we have restricted the sharing of information to only be truthful. This, on top of our game model simplifications, greatly reduce the complexity of modeling the knowledge of agents in the game to a point where we believe it to be realistic for us to model.
